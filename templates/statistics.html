About ESKtides


What is ESKtides:


ESKtides is a comprehensive database on ESKAPE derived peptides. We systematically incorporating all public ESKAPE genome datasets with strains and phages samples, as well as related datasets are NCBI, phageDB, MVP, Virus-Host and phast (Enterococcus faecium, Staphylococcus aureus, Klebsiella pneumoniae, Acinetobacter baumannii, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, Escherichia coli, Enterobacter cloacae, Enterococcus faecium Enterobacter aerogenes and their phages). In total, we re-annotate 5,630 ESKAPE strains and 1,179 ESKAPE phages by curating high-throughput datasets from those strains. We defined active ESKAPE derived peptides with Deeplearning method(LSTM) in each group as active peptides. Among them, there are 22,091,402 active peptides in our database from strains and phages. We also provide online prediction as our tools. Combined the final information, we found 12,067,248 active peptides are high level.


In ESKtides, users can:

  • Browse or search ESKAPE strains and phages annotation and filter by user-defined;
  • Browse or search ESKAPE derived peptides and find out peptides sequences;
  • Online tools for peptides mining, peptides phylogenetic tree and peptides chemical properties;
  • Download the ESKtides analysis results of a total of ESKAPE datasets both in strains and phages.
  • Download page contains the different levels ESKAPE drived peptides in ESKAPE strains and phages.
